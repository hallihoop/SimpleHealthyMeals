FAQ's

How much do you usually spend on groceries?

  1. The average American spends roughly between $250 - $300 a month on groceries. And that’s for one person alone!
  2. For me, I spend around $100-$150 a month.
  3. And even spending that much less, I am eating plenty every single day.

How do you make food last?

  1. Freezers are your best friend! I usually buy meat in bulk, so that I can freeze it.
  2. I will take the food I bought in bulk and separate it into ziplock bags to freeze. Learning how to effectively prep and freeze your food will save you so much time and cost.
  3. I end up with so much food that is prepped. Your food will last if you prepare in advance.
